Summary

I am a PIP2 Manager qualified Detective Inspector specialising in digital investigations with a demonstrated history of working in criminal investigations and offender management within law enforcement.

I am a clear and engaging communicator with good presentation skills, including having to frequently visualise and explain complex data in simplistic terms. I present to a range of audiences including students, experienced police officers, professional colleagues and senior stakeholders.

I have completed a Master's degree focusing on policing's adaptation and adoption of digital investigative tactics in criminal investigation. This was completed to distinction level and has been published and presented at an international academic conference. I have lectured at MSc level to students on a Cyber Security MSc course. I am currently studying on an MSc Computing Science course.

I hold a number of ICT, ICT Network, Cyber Security and Cyber Security Analysis qualifications alongside the Home Office Communications Data Investigator accreditation and associated communications data analysis and open source accreditations.

I was responsible for establishing and leading Norfolk Constabulary's Digital Investigation Team. I am currently the Technical Lead on the national TOEX team.
